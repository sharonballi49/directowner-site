How DirectOwner Works

DirectOwner is an owner‑to‑owner marketplace for cars and RVs. No middlemen. No dealership markups. Phase 1 is intentionally simple and verified manually for trust.

Browse Listings Sell Your Vehicle
Phase 1: Listings are reviewed manually before posting. Buyer‑seller contact is supported with safety in mind.

For Buyers

  1. Browse listings from verified owners.
  2. Open a listing and review details, photos, and title status where available.
  3. Contact the owner (Phase 1 contact may be coordinated by DirectOwner for safety).
  4. Optional financing: explore third‑party financing options if needed.
  5. Complete the purchase directly with the owner (inspection recommended).

For Sellers

  1. Submit your listing with vehicle details and photos.
  2. Verification & review to reduce fraud and build trust.
  3. Listing goes live and buyers can request contact.
  4. Sell directly — you control the deal and final price.
  5. Optional financing can help expand your buyer pool.

Trust & Safety

  1. Verified sellers (identity/ownership checks in Phase 1).
  2. Transparent expectations — we don’t act as dealer or lender.
  3. Scam prevention — report suspicious activity any time.
  4. Meet safely — public locations, inspection, secure payment methods.

Disclaimer: DirectOwner is a marketplace connecting private buyers and sellers. We are not a dealer or lender. Financing is offered by third‑party partners where available. Transactions occur directly between buyer and seller.
