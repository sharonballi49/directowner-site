How Selling Works

  1. Submit your listing using the Google Form (details + photos).
  2. Review & verification (Phase 1 is manual): basic checks to reduce fraud.
  3. We publish your listing on DirectOwner once approved.
  4. You sell directly to the buyer — no middleman. You control the deal.
What to prepare: Year/Make/Model, mileage, asking price, location, clear photos, and a condition summary.
