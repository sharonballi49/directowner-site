Frequently Asked Questions
How do I list my car or RV?

Go to the Sell page and submit your information through the listing form. Phase 1 reviews listings manually before they are published.

Do you handle payments or title transfer?

No. DirectOwner is a marketplace connecting private buyers and sellers. Transactions happen directly between the parties.

Are you a dealer or lender?

No. We are not a dealership or lender. Financing, where available, is provided by independent third-party partners.

How do I report a suspicious listing?

Email support@getdirectowner.com with the listing link and a short explanation of the issue.

Disclaimer: DirectOwner is a marketplace connecting private buyers and sellers. We are not a dealer, broker, or lender. Financing is offered by third-party partners where available. Transactions occur directly between buyer and seller.
